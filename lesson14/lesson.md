# Lesson 14: Deploying Your Gradio App Online 

## Friendly Introduction

Welcome to Lesson 14! Now you’ll learn how to make your Gradio app available to anyone in the world. Deploying means putting your app online so others can use it from their computers or phones.

## What You Will Learn
- What deployment means
- How to deploy your Gradio app for free
- How to share your app with others

## Real-Life Analogy
Imagine you made a cool drawing and want to show it to your friends. You hang it up in a public place so everyone can see it. Deploying your app is like hanging up your drawing online!

## Step-by-Step Explanation

### Step 1: What is Deployment?
Deployment means putting your app on the internet. Anyone with the link can use it.

### Step 2: Use Gradio’s Free Sharing Feature
Gradio makes it easy to share your app. When you run your app, Gradio gives you a link that starts with `https://`. You can send this link to anyone.

### Step 3: Use Hugging Face Spaces for Permanent Hosting
For a more permanent solution, you can use Hugging Face Spaces. It’s a website where you can upload your Gradio app and share it with the world.

#### How to Deploy with Hugging Face Spaces
1. Go to https://huggingface.co/spaces
2. Click "Create new Space"
3. Choose "Gradio" as the app type
4. Upload your app files (like `app.py`)
5. Click "Create"
6. Your app will get a public link you can share

### Full Code Example
Use any of your previous app files, like `app.py`.

### What Happens When We Run It
When you run your app locally, Gradio gives you a link to share. When you upload to Hugging Face Spaces, your app is online for anyone to use!
